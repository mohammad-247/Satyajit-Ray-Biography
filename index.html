Satyajit Ray

Basic Information

Full Name: Satyajit Ray
Born: May 2, 1921
Birthplace: Calcutta (now Kolkata), West Bengal, India
Died: April 23, 1992 (aged 70)
Nationality: Indian
Occupations: Film Director, Writer, Screenwriter, Illustrator, Music Composer, Calligrapher, Graphic Designer

Overview

Satyajit Ray was an Indian filmmaker, writer, and artist who is widely regarded as one of the greatest directors in world cinema. His films are known for their simplicity, emotional realism, and deep observation of human life. He played a major role in shaping Indian cinema on the international stage and is often mentioned alongside masters like Akira Kurosawa, Ingmar Bergman, and Federico Fellini.
